About Us

Formed in 2023, Sonika9 blends vintage cartoon‑inspired melodies with cosmic funk rhythms. The band members met at a local jam session and bonded over a shared love for kaleidoscopic art and deep grooves.

Members:

  • Terence Tanielu – Vocals & Guitar
  • Bryce Enos – Keys
  • Nate Bartole – Bass
  • Carlos Macias – Drums
